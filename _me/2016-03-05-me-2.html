Choice of music is arbitrary for me. I do not prefer any genre more over the other. Here are some of my favourite bands and artists:

Oasis were an English rock band formed by Gallagher brothers. Simply put, they were the best! I think Noel Gallagher is the greatest songwriter of this generation. Oasis - Live Forever, D'you know what I mean?

God is an Astronaut is a Post-Rock band from Ireland. Listening to their soothing but thrilling sound makes it easy to concentrate even when you are working on some serious stuff. [1] [2] [3] [4] [5].
